| UT Medical School has a 3D printer core facility that can make print outs of cortical surface models. Contact the core facility for this (Director: Billy Gill or his grad student Kevin Aroom).  The surface files should be in STL format. Convert as follows:   ```  mris_convert lh.pial lh.pial.stl ```   There are also numerous web sites and printers that can make the print out. cube x printer <http://cubify.com/Products/CubeXTechSpecs> |
