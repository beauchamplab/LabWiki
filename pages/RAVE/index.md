**R**eproducible **A**nalysis and **V**isualization of intracranial **E**EG (RAVE) is free and open-source software for the analysis of intracranial electroencephalogram (iEEG) data, including data collected using strips and grids (electrocorticography, ECoG) and depth electrodes (stereotactic EEG). A sister package to RAVE is the [YAEL electrode localization toolkit](../YAEL/index.md "YAEL").
